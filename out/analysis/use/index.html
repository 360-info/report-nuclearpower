The data here is from the International Renewable Energy Agency.

The tables (which can be updated by downloading them from IRENASTAT and placing them in data as irena-src-[measure][grid_status].csv) are:

  • Installed electricity capacity by country/area (MW) by Country/area, Technology, Grid connection and Year
  • Electricity generation (GWh) by Country/area, Technology, Grid connection and Year
